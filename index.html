Personal Info

Hi, I am Brady Greco and I am a full stack developer living in Orange County, CA.
     I recently completed an accelerated web development program at LearningFuze where I was able to learn the tools needed to feed my passion for development. I've been intrigued by programming ever since I was around 12 years old seeing ASCII art being scrolled in an AOL chat room. It amazed me that someone could just make an application like that because they wanted to! It wasn't until recently I decided to pursue a career in web development and it has been an amazing journey so far. Going from having very limited and basic knowledge to having the tools necessary to create anything I put my mind to has not only been challenging, but more rewarding than anything else I have done in my career.
     When I'm not working on my projects or pursuing new programming skills, you'll most likely find me on a lake fishing, in the kitchen cooking, or working on a puzzle. Please check out some of the projects I have been working on! If you're looking for someone who is passionate about development and hungry to learn, you can contact me via the "contact me" form or sending me an email.
